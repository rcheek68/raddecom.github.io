About
Raddecom Solutions

With over 40 years experience in the Nuclear Sector we are providing design solutions for complex decommissioning problems with cost effective, robust, flexible, sustainable products. We work with manufacturers supporting the nuclear sector to provide differentiated products that showcase UK manufacturing.
In addition Raddecom Solutions offers  Management Services through sector support, Product Concept Development and Product Design

Our products include the  "Con-tain Range" of Self Shielded Concrete Containers, Shielding Blocks, Shielding Bricks, Fast deployment Interim Storage Facilities (ISF) and development and optimisation of customer specified bespoke or existing designs and products
