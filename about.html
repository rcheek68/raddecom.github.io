About Us

RADDECOM SOLUTIONS has over 43 years experience in the Nuclear Sector and in collaboration with key partners we are providing design solutions for complex decommissioning problems with cost effective, robust, flexible, sustainable concrete waste containers for the nuclear sector. RADDECOM SOLUTIONS work alongside manufacturers supporting the development of differentiated products from concept to manufacture.

RADDECOM SOLUTIONS have developed the "Con-tain Range" of Self Shielded Concrete Containers a number of which have been selected by waste consigners for storage, transport and disposal of their radioactive wastes. We have also supported development of Shielding Blocks, Shielding Bricks and Fast deployment Interim Storage Facilities (ISF's) and offer development and optimisation of related customer specified bespoke or existing designs and products.

Who Are We?

Who makes up the team that has introduced a new range of Concrete Containers to the Nuclear Sector?

RADDECOM SOLUTIONS is lead by John Cheek who has been in the Nuclear Sector for over 43 years and is responsible for the design development of the concrete container range

John has been instrumental in championing and delivering new nuclear container strategies in the UK, John also has UK & international experience in decommissioning and waste management from concept to delivery in the field. He has developed concrete products for complex, extreme environments that are produced to the most demanding quality and manufacturing standards and has developed over 50 registered designs of which a number have been prototyped and manufactured and are in use in the field.
