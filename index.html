Raddecom Solutions

Concrete Waste Containers for Nuclear Facility Decommissioning

​RADDECOM SOLUTIONS is the trading name of consultant John Cheek who has developed a proven range of multi-purpose concrete waste container designs for storage, transport and disposal of Intermediate Level  Wastes (ILW) in response to UK and overseas markets seeking low cost, safe decommissioning, waste processing and waste packaging solutions for nuclear facilities and plants.

RADDECOM SOLUTIONS has registered designs for over 50 containers of which a number have been selected by waste consigners for manufacture.  RADDECOM SOLUTIONS have and are providing bespoke customer driven and novel solutions that deliver a range of benefits and cost savings that other market suppliers are not able to match.
